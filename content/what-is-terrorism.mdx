title: "What Is Terrorism?"
description: "An academic definition of terrorism and the principles that guide responsible study of the topic."
---

Terrorism is the use or threat of violence to create fear and influence political, social, or ideological goals. In academic settings, you should approach this topic with factual context, careful language, and respect for public safety.

<Note>
  A careful definition matters because it helps students focus on behavior, intent, and public safety rather than stereotypes about religion, nationality, race, or identity.
</Note>

## Study principles

These three principles guide how you should engage with the topic throughout this resource.
